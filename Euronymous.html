Øystein Aarseth (Euronymous)

Founder of Black Metal

Here's a time line of Euronymous:

  • 1968 - Born in Surnadal, Norway
  • 1984 - formed Mayhem along with bassist Necrobutcher (Jørn Stubberud) and drummer Kjetil Manheim.
  • 1986 - recorded the Metalion in the Park demo under the name Checker Patrol, Metalion
  • 1988 - Per "Dead" Ohlin became Mayhem's vocalist and Jan Axel "Hellhammer" Blomberg became its drummer.
  • 1991 - Opened a record shop named "Helvete"(Norwegian for "hell")
  • 1992 - Burn the Fantoft Stave Church in Bergen (for fun)
  • 1993 - Varg Vikernes stabbed Euronymous to death at his apartment in Oslo.

I'm a religious person and I will fight those who misuse His name. People are not supposed to believe in themselves and be individualists. They are supposed to OBEY, to be the SLAVES of religion"
